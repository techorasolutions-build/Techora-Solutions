FAQ

Questions we get asked
all the time

Everything you need to know before getting started with Techora.

How long does it take to build my website? +
Most websites are ready within 5–7 working days. For larger projects like college portals or multi-page shops, it may take up to 10–14 days. We always give you a clear timeline upfront.
Do I need to buy a domain or hosting separately? +
We guide you through the entire process. Domain and hosting can be purchased through us or independently — we help you set it all up. Annual hosting typically costs ₹2,000–₹5,000/year depending on your needs.
Can I update the website myself after it's built? +
Yes! We offer a simple admin panel option (Premium plan) and also provide basic training. For smaller updates like text, images or pricing changes, you can simply WhatsApp us and we'll update it for you — fast.
What if I don't like the design? +
No problem at all. We offer unlimited revisions until you're 100% satisfied. We share a live preview before anything goes live, so you can request changes freely.
Do you work with businesses outside your city? +
Absolutely. We work with clients across India — everything is handled online via WhatsApp, email, and video call. Distance is never a barrier.
Is there any hidden charge after the website is delivered? +
Never. The price you see is the price you pay. Domain renewal and hosting are the only recurring costs, and those are transparently communicated before you sign up.
